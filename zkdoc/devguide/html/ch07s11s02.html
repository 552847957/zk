Column Headers

You could specify the column headers by use of listhead and listheader as follows[38]. In addition to label, you could specify an image as the header by use of the image property.

<listbox width="200px">
    <listhead>    
        <listheader label="Name"/>        
        <listheader label="Occupation"/>        
    </listhead>    
...
</listbox>

[38] This feature is a bit different from XUL, where listhead and listheader are used.
